Generative AI in Healthcare — Evidence Overview (2024–2025)

Adoption · Applications · Mediators · Outcomes · Risks · Global Programs

Applications Mediators Outcomes Risks Global

Adoption (2024–2025)

  • 86% of health orgs report some AI use (HIMSS 2024).
  • 100% (43/43) U.S. systems piloting ambient notes (Poon et al., 2025).
  • Imaging AI in ~90% of systems (late-2024 surveys).

Applications (model/vendor examples)

  • Documentation (ambient scribes): Nuance DAX Copilot, Abridge, TORTUS (UK), Suki.
  • Patient communication: GPT-4 drafts integrated with Epic In Basket.
  • Decision support / medical QA: Med-PaLM 2, tuned GPT-4, retrieval-augmented QA.

Mediators (implementation factors)

  • Workflow fit & clinician training drive value.
  • Human oversight & sign-off; audit trail.
  • Validation frameworks: NHS ambient-scribe (2025); FDA SaMD AI/ML; WHO LMM guidance.

Outcomes (quantified)

  • Efficiency: ~16k clinician hours saved; ~7,260 users (Kaiser, 15 mo).
  • Experience/throughput: +23.5% face-to-face; −8.2% visit length (NHS London pilot).
  • Communication: 78.5% high-quality; 45.1% empathetic (Ayers JAMA IM 2023).

Risks & safeguards

  • Hallucination (medical citation tasks): GPT-4 ≈ 28.6%; GPT-3.5 ≈ 39.6%.
  • Misinformation, bias, privacy & consent risks.
  • Mitigate with: RAG over curated corpora; adversarial tests; DPIAs; subgroup metrics; post-market monitoring.

Global programs (examples)

  • NHS England: ambient-scribe guidance; screening testbeds; A&E throughput gains.
  • United States: Epic GPT-4 In-Basket & ambient deployments.
  • Asia-Pacific: Ping An telehealth; Australian scribe pilots.
Sources (examples): HIMSS/Medscape Analytics 2024; Poon et al., NPJ Digit Med 2025; NHS England ambient-scribe guidance 2025; Kaiser Permanente deployment updates 2025; Ayers et al., JAMA Internal Medicine 2023; JMIR hallucination accuracy 2024; WHO LMM 2025; FDA SaMD AI/ML.
